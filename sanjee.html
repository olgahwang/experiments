When we are young

All men have stars, but they are not the same things for different people. For some, who are travelers, the stars are guides. For others they are no more than little lights in the sky. For others, who are scholars, they are problems... But all these stars are silent. You-You alone will have stars as no one else has them.

I wish you will never stop wondering and exploring your starts. Always stay hungry, stay foolish.

We are full of fears: being imperfect, being not good enough, making something wrong, being too chaotic. Don't afraid of it. Be brave, embrace chaos and let it make something unique, real and fascinating.

Just remember all the incredible things you did. Remember how you always made something that seemed impossible, how you led people, made them believe and create. Remember those magical moments, that create piece by piece the life of Supernova Sanjee.

I wish you to have life full of dreams, fruitful experiences, interesing and different people. I wish you never stop growing, developing and creating. Be self, listen to your heart and be brave to strive for all the dreams you have.

Let the magic begin!
